USER FLOW (Simple)

Goal: turn a messy business problem into a validated decision + execution package.

Principles:
- Default rule owner: CLIENT.
- Client does NOT see PASS/FAIL; only interpretation.
- FAIL is diagnostic, not a client rejection.

Step 1 — Intake (15–30 min)
- Client describes the problem and constraints.
- Output: clear statement + owner + constraints.

Step 2 — Structure (30–60 min)
- Boundary, actors, interactions, contradiction, resources.
- Output: structured problem model.

Step 3 — Synthesis (30–60 min)
- Form selection, solution set, mechanisms.
- Output: decision draft + mechanisms.

Step 4 — Decision Lock (required)
- One decision is fixed.
- Rejected alternatives are fixed.
- Cost of error is fixed.

Step 5 — Decision & Plan (30–60 min)
- Final decision, implementation map, rule.
- Output: plan + operational rule.

Step 6 — Validation (5–10 min)
- Harness runs Gate0–Gate14.
- Client sees only:
  A) “Decision ready”
  B) “Requires mandatory input clarification”
  C) “Decision impossible under given conditions”

Step 7 — Delivery (10–15 min)
- Client receives report + plan + rule.

Total time (manual): ~2–4 hours per case.
